Hi! My name is Sydney Stusek I am a junior studying Sport Communication and Media with minors in Journalism and Digital Marketing. I enjoy capturing real emotions, authentic moments, and unique perspectives that tell a story. Photography gives me the chance to connect with people, explore new places, and express my creativity in ways that feel meaningful. I am always learning new techniques, experimenting with lighting, and finding ways to improve my work. Whether I’m photographing portraits, sports, or events, I aim to create images that feel natural, expressive, and full of life. My goal is to make every session enjoyable and comfortable so that my clients feel confident and excited about their photos.

Over the years, I’ve developed a style that focuses on natural lighting, candid expressions, and storytelling. I believe that the best photos come from genuine moments, not forced poses. I love working with clients to understand their ideas and bring their vision to life. Every person I photograph has their own personality, and I try to highlight that in every session. I also enjoy editing and enhancing photos in a way that keeps them true to the moment while adding a polished, professional touch. Photography is more than just a hobby for me—it’s something I hope to continue growing and sharing with others. I’m grateful for every opportunity to capture memories that people can look back on for years to come.

Fun Facts About Me
